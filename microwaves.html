What are Microwaves?

NASA and Microwaves

Microwaves

Microwaves have frequencies ranging from about 3 gigahertz up to about 30 trillion hertz, and wavelengths of about 30 centimeters to 3 millimeters. This means that they have a long wavelength and a low frequency.

How Microwaves Are Used

Internet

Both mobile phones and WiFi use microwaves to transmit information.

Speed Detection

Microwaves used by fixed traffic speed cameras, and for radar, which is used by aircraft, ships and weather forcasters.

Food

Microwaves are used to heat food quickly.
